About Us

Welcome to our film streaming platform, where
"Connecting Film-Lovers" is more than just a
slogan; it's our mission.

We're dedicated to supporting small filmmakers
and helping you discover interesting films from
around the world.

Dive into a diverse collection of cinematic gems
and connect with fellow movie enthusiasts.
Start exploring and embracing the love for film today.

SquareEyes was born from a shared love for film.

It all began with a group of avid movie enthusiasts
who envisioned a space where small filmmakers
could shine, and viewers could uncover hidden
cinematic treasures.

Today, SquareEyes is dedicated to supporting
independent filmmakers and curating a diverse
collection of compelling films from
around the globe.

Join us in celebrating the art
of filmmaking and the joy
of watching.
